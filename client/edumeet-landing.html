All Features

Everything a real classroom needs

A complete professional education suite. Every feature listed here is included, free, with no sign-up.

🎙
Pre-Join Lobby
Test camera, microphone, and speakers before entering. Select individual devices from dropdowns. See yourself in a live mirrored preview. No surprises when you join.
🖥
Full-Screen Share to All
When the instructor shares their screen, it instantly appears in full-screen spotlight mode for every student simultaneously. Students cannot dismiss or stop it — it's locked until the instructor ends the share.
Instructor only
🔇
Instructor Audio Controls
Mute a specific student with a single click on their attendance row. Or silence the entire class at once with "Mute All". Students are notified by name who muted them and can unmute themselves once permitted.
Instructor only
📋
Live Attendance Tracking
Every student is permanently recorded the moment they join — name, exact join time, and left time. Students who leave mid-class remain in the record, marked absent. Export the full list as a CSV spreadsheet anytime.
✋
Raise Hand System
Students raise their hand with one click. The instructor sees a live "Raised Hands" list in the attendance panel ordered by time, and receives an instant toast notification with the student's name. Lowering is one click.
💬
Group Class Chat
A real-time group chat shared by all participants — instructor and students alike. Messages travel over WebRTC DataChannels with a built-in message queue so no message is lost even if sent before the connection fully opens.
📊
Live Polls
Instructors create a poll with a custom question and up to any number of options. All students receive it as a pop-up card. Results update in real-time as votes come in, shown as animated percentage bars — visible to the instructor immediately.
Instructor only
📢
Broadcast Announcements
Instructors can send a full-class announcement that appears as a dismissible banner overlay on every student's screen simultaneously. Ideal for instructions, reminders, or important notices during a session.
Instructor only
🔄
Auto-Rejoin on Refresh
If a student accidentally refreshes or their browser reloads, EduMeet detects the previous session from storage and automatically returns them to the pre-join lobby with name and room pre-filled — rejoining in under 2 seconds. Intentional leaves are never affected.
🔗
One-Click Invite Links
The instructor copies the invite link from the top bar with one click. Students open the link and see only a name field — no role picker, no room ID to enter, no app to install. Works on any browser including mobile.
⏹
Instructor Session Control
Only the instructor can end the entire class for everyone. When "End Class" is clicked, a signal is sent to all students who receive a notification and are automatically returned to the home screen. Students can always leave individually at any time.
Instructor only
🔒
End-to-End Encrypted
All video, audio, and chat travels directly peer-to-peer using WebRTC with mandatory DTLS-SRTP encryption. The server only exchanges signaling messages (connection setup) — it never sees, stores, or has access to your class content.
Always on

Roles

Instructor vs Student

EduMeet has two distinct roles with clearly separated permissions. No student can accidentally access instructor tools.

🎓
Instructor
Creates the room · Full control
  • Share screen (full-screen for all students)
  • Mute individual students or the entire class
  • Mark all students present in attendance
  • Export attendance as CSV
  • Broadcast announcements to all students
  • Launch live polls with real-time results
  • End the class for all participants
  • See raised-hands list in real time
  • Cannot raise hand (has no hand-raise button)
👤
Student
Joins via invite link · Participant
  • Toggle own microphone on or off
  • Toggle own camera on or off
  • Raise hand to signal the instructor
  • Participate in group chat
  • Vote in instructor polls
  • Leave class individually at any time
  • Cannot share screen
  • Cannot mute others or end the class
  • Cannot dismiss instructor's screen share

FAQ

Common questions

The instructor copies the invite link from the meeting room (🔗 Invite button in the top bar) and shares it via any channel — WhatsApp, email, SMS, or LMS. Students open the link in any browser, enter only their name, and join immediately. No download, no account, no password.
No. All video, audio, and chat travel directly between participants (peer-to-peer) using WebRTC with DTLS-SRTP encryption. The server only handles signaling — the brief exchange of connection details needed to set up the call. It never sees your video, audio, or messages.
EduMeet saves the session to the browser's sessionStorage before the page unloads. On the next load it detects the saved session and automatically takes the student back to the pre-join lobby with their name and room pre-filled. They're back in the class in about 2 seconds. This only applies to accidental refreshes — clicking "Leave" clears the session so it doesn't trigger.
When an instructor mutes a student, their microphone track is disabled immediately and they receive a notification. They can unmute themselves by clicking the Mute button — the instructor cannot permanently prevent a student from unmuting. However, the instructor can mute them again. This is intentional: it mirrors real classroom etiquette without giving instructors invasive control.
When the instructor starts sharing their screen, all students immediately see it in a full-screen "spotlight" mode — the video tile grid is hidden and replaced with the instructor's screen. Students have no button to dismiss or stop it. The share ends when the instructor clicks "Stop Share" or closes the browser's native "Stop sharing" button.
Up to 500 participants per class. The limit is enforced server-side — if a 501st person tries to join, they receive a "room full" error. In practice, WebRTC mesh connections work best with smaller groups; for very large classes, students with cameras off reduce bandwidth significantly.
EduMeet is a separate service built into the SmartShare platform, accessible at /edu-meet.html. It has its own URL, its own pre-join lobby, its own meeting room UI, and its own feature set designed for education. It shares SmartShare's signaling server and design system, but its code and experience are completely independent from the main SmartShare file-sharing app.
